Faecal Sludge & Septage Management

Faecal Sludge & Septage Management (FSSM) prioritizes human excreta management, a waste stream with the highest potential for spreading diseases. It is a low-cost and easily scalable sanitation solution that focuses on safe collection, transportation, treatment, and reuse of human waste.
