Different types of waste management services that we are providing

EPR services

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) is defined in the Plastic Waste Management Rules 2016 as the responsibility of a producer for the environmentally sound management of the product until the end of its life.

SWM services

Solid waste management (SWM) refers to the process of collecting, treating, and disposal of solid waste. The Urban Local Bodies (ULBs)/ Municipalities are responsible for the management of municipal solid waste which is a challenging task.

City compost

Our city compost improves soil fertility and reduces chemical fertilizer usage resulting in cost savings for farmers. Besides, as the city compost (made from the processing of urban organic waste) reaches back to farms as a resource, the circularity in the food chain is achieved.
